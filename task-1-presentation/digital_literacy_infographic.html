What is Digital Literacy?

Digital Literacy is the ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies — responsibly, effectively, and ethically. It is not just knowing how to use a smartphone; it is knowing how to think critically in a digital world.

Key Areas to Master
🔒

Safe Internet Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords for every account
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
  • Recognize phishing links before clicking
  • Avoid banking on public Wi-Fi
  • Update apps and OS regularly
🌐

Professional Online Presence

  • Build a GitHub profile with projects
  • Keep LinkedIn Education section updated
  • Use a professional email for sign-ups
  • Think before you post — it stays forever
  • Keep personal and professional accounts separate
